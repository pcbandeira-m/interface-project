Who writes


Passionate about books and addicted to 'just one more chapter,' I’ve turned my bookshelf into my favorite topic. My comfort zone shifts between the magical realms of fantasy and the everyday dramas of the YA universe, but I never say no to a good romantic comedy to warm the heart. I believe that every book read offers a new perspective on the world, and it is this passion that drives me to write.

My goal is to create a space where readers can find their next favorite read, whether it’s a profound drama or an irresistible cliché. In addition to reviews, I share personalized lists and reflections on the literary life. If you also believe that dragons and clumsy encounters are the perfect combination for the weekend, this place is for you!
